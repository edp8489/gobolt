## Nut Factors
The following nut lubrication factors are used.

| Bolt Condition | K | Reference |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Black Oxide | 0.30 | 1 |
| Zinc-plated | 0.20 | 1 |
| Lubricated | 0.18 | 1 |
| Cadmium-plated | 0.16 | 1 |
| Bowman anti-seize | 0.12 | 1 |
